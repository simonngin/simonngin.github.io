Memory Games

Sequence Recall

Test your memory with color, number, and emoji sequences. Remember the pattern and repeat it back in the correct order.

Lost and Found

Track emoji items across a grid. Remember where each item was placed and find them when they disappear.

Maze Navigation

Follow the running man's path through a 6x6 maze. Remember the exact route to reach the treasure.

Card Matching

Classic memory card game with fruit symbols. Match pairs by remembering card positions on the grid.

Price Tag

Memorize grocery item prices and recall them accurately. Features a custom number keyboard for precise input.

Key Features

📈 Progressive Difficulty

Four difficulty levels from Easy to Expert. Unlock harder challenges as you build your 7-day streak and improve your skills.

🔥 Streak Tracking

Maintain daily challenges to build your streak. Premium users get a 3-day grace period to keep streaks alive.

🏆 Brain Score System

Earn points based on difficulty: Easy (1pt), Medium (2pts), Hard (3pts), Expert (5pts). Track your cognitive improvement over time.

🎖️ Achievement Badges

Unlock badges for milestones and achievements. Celebrate your progress with score-based rewards and streak accomplishments.

🔄 Unlimited Retries

Practice makes perfect! Retry challenges as many times as needed, with new patterns generated after each attempt.

📱 Offline Play

All games work offline with local storage. No internet connection required for daily brain training sessions.
