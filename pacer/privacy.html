Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 23, 2025
Privacy Summary: Pacer prioritizes your privacy. We collect minimal data (location during runs only), store nothing permanently on our servers, use no external analytics or tracking, and keep all user preferences locally on your device.

This Privacy Policy describes how Pacer ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Data

What we collect: GPS location coordinates during active workout sessions

Why we collect it: Essential for calculating running pace, distance, and providing real-time audio feedback

When we collect it: Only when you actively start a run and grant location permission

How long we keep it: Location data is used in real-time for calculations and is NOT stored permanently

1.2 User Preferences (Stored Locally)

The following information is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults:

  • Display name or username
  • Subscription status (free or pro)
  • Custom pace reminder intervals (Pro users only)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Health app permission status

Important: This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

1.3 Health Data Integration (Optional)

What we access: When you grant permission, we write workout data to Apple Health/HealthKit

Data written: Workout type (running), start time, end time, duration, distance

Your control: This is entirely optional and can be disabled in iOS Settings. We do not read existing health data.

1.4 Subscription Information

What we collect: Subscription status and purchase receipts

How it's handled: Managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We cache subscription status locally for app functionality.

Payment processing: All payments are processed by Apple; we do not handle payment information directly.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • Location data: Real-time pace calculations, distance tracking, audio coaching feedback
  • User preferences: Personalizing your app experience, remembering settings
  • Subscription status: Enabling Pro features for subscribers

2.2 Apple Health Integration

  • Writing completed workout summaries to HealthKit (when permission granted)
  • Allowing you to track fitness progress across apps

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage Only

All user data and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain user databases or cloud storage for personal information.

3.2 Location Data Processing

  • GPS data is processed in real-time on your device
  • Location accuracy filtering: readings with >20m accuracy are automatically discarded
  • No location history is maintained or transmitted
  • Background location is enabled only during active runs

3.3 Network Communications

The App communicates with Apple's servers only for:

  • Subscription verification and management
  • App Store product information
  • No user personal data is transmitted in these communications

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Apple Services

We integrate with the following Apple services:

  • Core Location: GPS tracking during workouts
  • HealthKit: Optional workout data storage
  • StoreKit: Subscription management
  • AVFoundation: Audio speech synthesis for coaching

4.2 What We Don't Use

No External Analytics: We do not use Firebase, Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or any similar tracking services

No Crash Reporting: We do not use Crashlytics or similar crash reporting services

No Advertising: We do not integrate with advertising networks or collect data for ads

No Social Media: We do not integrate with social media platforms

5. Your Privacy Rights and Controls

5.1 Location Permissions

  • You can deny location access (app will display appropriate messaging)
  • Change location permissions in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services
  • App requires "While Using App" permission for core functionality

5.2 Health Data Control

  • Health integration is completely optional
  • Manage permissions in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health
  • You can revoke access at any time without affecting core app functionality

5.3 Subscription Management

  • Cancel subscriptions in iOS Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions
  • Subscription data is managed by Apple, not stored on our servers

6. Data Retention

6.1 Location Data

GPS location data is used only during active workout sessions for real-time calculations and is not retained after the workout ends.

6.2 User Preferences

Stored locally on your device until you delete the app or reset your device.

6.3 Health Data

Once written to Apple Health, data is managed according to Apple's HealthKit policies and your iOS settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Pacer is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. International Users

If you are using Pacer outside your country of residence, please note that your information may be processed in accordance with local laws. All data processing follows Apple's iOS data handling standards.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy
  • Providing notice through the App or App Store updates
  • For material changes, providing advance notice where possible

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through:

  • App Store support channels
  • In-app feedback options

11. Compliance

This privacy policy is designed to comply with:

  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • iOS privacy requirements
  • General privacy best practices

Key Takeaway: Pacer is designed with privacy by design principles. We collect only the minimum data necessary for functionality, store nothing permanently on servers, and give you full control over your data and permissions.
