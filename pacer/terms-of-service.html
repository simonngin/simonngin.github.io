2. Description of Service

Pacer is your personal pace coach — a lightweight, simple tool designed to help you maintain perfect pace during your runs. Whether you're training for a 5K, pushing for a personal best, or just want to stay on track, Pacer provides:

  • Real-Time Pace Tracking: Instant updates on your pace as you run
  • Smart Audio Coaching: Voice prompts keep you informed without checking your phone
  • Clean, Simple Interface: No clutter, just the essentials for better running
  • Apple Health Integration: Save your runs and view all workouts in one place
  • Pro Features: Custom pace targets and reminder intervals (subscription required)

Pacer is built to be lightweight, beautiful, and effective — so you can focus on your run, not your phone.
